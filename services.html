About Us

We, Jenny and Martina are sisters from Sligo,Ireland. Growing up we always had a passion for cleaning, it was always something we loved to do together. As kids we would offer to clean for neighbours and family. Four years ago we decided to take the plunge and turn our passion into a business and the rest is history. We now have a team of ten amazing women who will ensure that all of our clients will get the signature Squeaky Clean experinece.
